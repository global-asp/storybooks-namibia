Mi ama My body

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter

Kileni A. Fernando

Ju|’hoansi

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Mi !aah /xoa ka.

I can run with it.

Mi khu /xoa ka.

I can jump with it.

Mi djxani /xoa ka.

I can dance with it.

Mi djxaa /xoa ka.

I can swim with it.

Mi khu /xoa ka.

I can skip with it.

Mi //u /xoa ka.

I can kick with it.

Mi dz’ang /xoa ka.

I can escape with it.

Xabe mi /oa n!ohm /xoa ka.

But I can never fly with it.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Kileni A. Fernando
Language: Ju|’hoansi
Level: Level 1
Source: My body from African Storybook
