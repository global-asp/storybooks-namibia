Hoqrokhoea he gǂa’in.

This dress is long.

Jesia he nǃa’an.

This jersey is big.

ǀHaia he nǃa’an.

This bag is big.

Xorea he…

This belt is…

ǁAh he tzema.

This hat is small.

Sokisih ǃoa’mh.

The socks are short.

Xabe gǃoeh sa he o hi zesin.

But these shoes are new.

Siǃa too te ǂ’aun.

… they fit well.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
