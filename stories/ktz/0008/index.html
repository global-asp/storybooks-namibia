Hatce re a du? What are you doing?

Nina Orange

Wiehan de Jager

Cwi Debe

Ju|’hoansi

Level 1

Mi ge’e.

I am singing.

Ha ui.

She is waving.

Mi ǁ’am.

I am clapping.

Ha gǃkun.

She is stretching.

Ha ǃ’au.

He is calling.

Mi mani.

I am answering.

Ha ǂaeǂae.

She is listening.

Hatce re a du?

What are you doing?

Written by: Nina Orange
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Cwi Debe
Language: Ju|’hoansi
Level: Level 1
Source: What are you doing? from African Storybook
