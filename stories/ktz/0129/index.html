Da’ama!oa /k’a /k’ana Lazy little brother

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter

Sylvia Fernandu

Juǀʼhoan

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Mi ≠xai ka du da’a.

I wake up and make fire.

Mi n≠ai khuia g!u.

I boil some water.

Mi //’hom da’a.

I chop the firewood.

Mi honi kxo.

I stir the pot.

Mi //xai samende !o.

I sweep the floor.

Mi //ka’a taansi.

I wash the dishes.

Hatce khoea re mi //koa tca ko g/aoh ko //’aea mi tsihn kui?

Why do I work so hard… … when my brother is busy playing?
