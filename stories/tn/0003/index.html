Diaparo tsa sekolo School clothes

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter

Lorato Trok

Setswana

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Mosese o o motelele.

This dress is long.

Jeresi e e kgolo.

This jersey is big.

Sekgwama se se segolo.

This bag is big.

Lebanta le…..

This belt is…

Hutshe e ennye.

This hat is small.

Dikauso di dikhutshwane.

The socks are short.

Mme fela ditlhako tse di dintšhwa.

But these shoes are new.

…zingena kahle.

… they fit well.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Lorato Trok
Language: Setswana
Level: Level 1
