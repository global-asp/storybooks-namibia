Omerimwino Feelings

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Kathy Arbuckle, Ingrid Schechter

Angelika Tjouṱuku, Asnath Mundjindjiri

Otjiherero

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Me rimunu oviṋa ovingi momutima.

My heart feels a lot of things.

Ami mbi yoroka Tjikuukae tji me tu serekarere ovihambarere ngurova.

I feel happy when my granny tells us stories in the evening.

Mbi rimuna ouyova tji me nyanda pamwe nepanga randje.

I feel silly when I play with my friend.

Ami mbi hihamwa omutima tate tja tja ke novimariva.

I feel bad when my dad says he does not have money.

Mbi rimuna okusuverwa mama tje rivaranga mu ami.

I feel loved when my mom gives me a hug.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Kathy Arbuckle, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Angelika Tjouṱuku, Asnath Mundjindjiri
Language: Otjiherero
Level: Level 1
