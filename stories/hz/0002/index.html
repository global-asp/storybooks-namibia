Tara ovipuka Look at the animals

Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

Angelika Tjoutuku, Asnath Mundjindjiri

Otjiherero

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Tara ovipuka.

Look at the animals.

Ongombe i tja “Mbuu.”

The cow says, “Moo.”

Ongombo i tja “Mee, mee.”

The goat says, “Meh, meh.”

Okakambe ke tja “Hihih.”

The horse says, “Neigh.”

Ombinda i tja”Hwii, hwiii.”

The pig says, “Grunt.”

Ohunguriva i tja “Kurukukuu.”

The chicken says, “Cluck.”

Ombwa i tja “Hau, hau.”

The dog says, “Woof.”

Omuṱuta u tja “Tjtj!”

The farmer says, “Shhh!”

Written by: Jenny Katz
Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Angelika Tjoutuku, Asnath Mundjindjiri
Language: Otjiherero
Level: Level 1
