Buke zopumbompepo Weather book

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter

Valentin Mpasi

Rukwangali

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Kuna kukuta.

It is dry.

Ezuva upyu unene.

The sun is hot.

Mpepo kuna ku pepa.

It is windy.

Muremo guna kara ko.

It is cloudy.

Mufu guna kara ko.

It is cold.

Mvhura kuna kuroka.

It is raining.

Nombadi dina kara ko.

There is thunder.

Ame tani mona ekongoro.

I see a rainbow.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Valentin Mpasi
Language: Rukwangali
Level: Level 1
Source: Weather book from African Storybook
