Thuli Kwa kara nonohuki dononsupi.

Thuli has short hair.

Anna Kwa kara nonohuki dononde.

Anna has long hair.

Cathy Kwa kara nonohuki dononde unene.

Cathy has the longest hair.

Zama ana takere nohuki dendi.

Zama has plaited her hair.

Baba Kwa kara nononzwedu.

Baba has a beard.

Zanele ana fufura nohuki dendi.

Zanele has combed her hair.

Thabo ana Tete nohuki dendi.

Thabo has cut his hair.

Themba ana kurura nohuki dendi.

Themba has shaved his hair.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Anna Sengumbe
Language: Rukwangali
Level: Level 1
Source: Hair from African Storybook
